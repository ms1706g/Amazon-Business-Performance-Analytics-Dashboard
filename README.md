# Amazon Business Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Amazon sales performance, customer engagement, and product trends. The dashboard provides actionable business insights through KPI monitoring, sales trend analysis, category performance evaluation, and customer review tracking.

## Business Problem

Organizations require real-time visibility into sales performance, product demand, and customer engagement metrics to make data-driven decisions. This dashboard consolidates key business metrics into a single analytical view.

## Key Metrics

* Total Sales: $2.18M+
* Products Sold: 27K+
* Customer Reviews: 19M+
* Category-wise Performance Analysis
* Monthly and Quarterly Sales Trends
* Product-Level Insights

## Features

* Executive KPI Dashboard
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Product Category Performance Tracking
* Customer Review Analytics
* Interactive Filtering and Drill-Down Reporting
* Business Performance Monitoring

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel
* Data Cleaning
* Business Analytics
* KPI Reporting

## Dashboard Insights

* Identified top-performing product categories.
* Analyzed sales trends across multiple periods.
* Evaluated customer engagement using review metrics.
* Supported business decision-making through visual reporting.
